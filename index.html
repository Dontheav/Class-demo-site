    About Me

    Donovan Ruddock is a versatile professional with a background in customer service and a recent focus on computer science. Born in Jamaica and later moving to Canada, Ruddock has transitioned from a successful career in professional boxing to pursuing opportunities in technology and education.

    After his boxing career, which spanned from 1982 to 2015 and included notable fights against Mike Tyson and Lennox Lewis, Ruddock has demonstrated his adaptability by obtaining an Associate of Arts degree in Computer Science from Palm Beach State College in 2023. He has also earned certifications in computer programming, telecommunications, and CPR, showcasing his commitment to continuous learning and skill development.

    Ruddock's diverse work experience includes roles such as a substitute teacher and paraprofessional, customer service representative, college retention coach, and referee. His ability to work in various environments, from classrooms to retail settings, highlights his interpersonal skills and adaptability.

    Currently residing in Weeki Wachi, Florida, Ruddock is seeking opportunities to apply his customer service expertise and newly acquired computer science knowledge in a professional setting.
